My Experience

An experience that I will never forget is that I was able to complete the final year project
in the IMD314 course. This program is known as the Outreach Program titled Museum Attack. This
program is located at the Terengganu State Museum and takes place on 11-12 January 2023. For this
program, I hold the position of Treasurer. The treasurer's job is very challenging because with the
consent of the public they have entrusted me to manage the finances. I learned a lot in this program,
for example I as the treasurer have to calculate the budget for this program. I also deal with the
Treasurer and HEA to approve spending money for the Museum Attack program. I gained a lot of
experience and knowledge throughout this program
